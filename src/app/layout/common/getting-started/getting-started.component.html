Getting Started
  1. 1
    Verify your Channel and Add a Video Introduction
    Complete your profile by adding a video introduction and verifying your channel.
    Post a campaign to get free quotes
    We make it easy for you to Post a campaing in 4 easy steps. Write a detailed description of your requirements, choose your selection criteria, set a budget and voila! Your campaign is live, you can see proposals coming in while relaxing in your armchair!
  2. 2
    Get invited or apply to Live campaigns
    Once you have updated your profile, you are all set for getting invitations from brands for their campaigns and you can also be a little proactive and apply to campaigns yourself.
    Go check out the Live Campaigns section today and get started with your first campaign.
    Evaluate Influencers
    BookMyInfluencers shows you channel statistics such as Engagement Rate, Collaboration Score, Performance Score (Show meaning on hover) that help you in selecting the right channel for your brand and since you deal directly with the influencers, you get the best pricing too! Searching Influencers has never been this easy.
  3. 3
    Discuss and negotiate terms with brands
    Great, you have a marketing campaign in your kitty, use BookMyInfluencers platform to communicate seamlessly with brands, discuss campaign briefs, negotiate pricing and set timelines as per your convenience before you even begin working on the script.
    Once the order is placed, use the communication channel in case you have any changes you’d like to discuss with the brand.
    Work Together
    Connect with influencers with a click of a button. Choose to call, chat or email them and once you place an order, BookMyInfluencers gives you regular updates on the order milestones, so that you don’t have to worry about your campaign.
  4. 4
    Deliver the video and get paid
    You focus on putting your best work forward and timely delivery of the video project.
    BookMyInfluencers secures the payment from the brand in advance and transfers it to your account after successful delivery of the campaign video. It is as simple as that!.
    Secure Payments through BookMyInfluencers
    The order amount is secured in an escrow till the order is completed as per your requirements. The payment is released to the influencer after the video is live.
  5. 5
    BookMyInfluencer Support
    Well we have tried to make the platform as friendly and convenient to use for all the stakeholders but we understand there still might be some doubts in your mind. Worry not, we got you covered! Use the chat option on the bottom right of your page whenever you want to share something and our Support team will be with you soon to help you with anything and everything to enhance your BMI experience! :)
